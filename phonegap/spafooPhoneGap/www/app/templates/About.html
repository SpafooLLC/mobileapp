About Spafoo

On site hair and body services from your phone

SpaFoo offers personal service providers the opportunity to join the digital revolution! If you are a professionally licensed cosmetologist, esthetician or massage therapist, imagine a new way to practice your profession and grow your business. Our digital platform allows you the mobility and flexibility you need while our platform helps you grow your beauty business.

At SpaFoo.com, we offer clients an "at your service" approach to personal care, while we encourage and support beauty industry professionals with the ease of a smart phone app. This is a revolutionary business model for the beauty industry professional who does not want to be tied to a traditional salon.

Once registered into the system, your services will be available to all SpaFoo users. These users are your clients who will request services on an as-needed basis. The service provider receives the request as a pop up based on hair type, location, time and service necessary.

Marketing and scheduling, often a challenge for an independent beauty professional, is built into the SpaFoo concierge platform. Now is an opportunity to grow your business independent of a traditional, brick and mortar salon. Better yet, you receive a greater ratio of your market rate. Weekly payments for your services are electronically deposited directly into your bank account.

Join the beauty revolution. Join SpaFoo today!
